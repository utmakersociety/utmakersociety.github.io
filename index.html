Getting to Us

The Maker Space is located in North Engineering 1042.

To organize and operate an educational makerspace at the University of Toledo by providing the community with the tools, knowledge and space they need to create their dreams and propel their future. We cater to all students regardless of personal or educational background by promoting diversity and individuality of thought to create a community of Makers who can each contribute and develop their unique skills. We provide students with a platform to expand their leadership experience and allow them to apply their experience to real-world engineering projects.
