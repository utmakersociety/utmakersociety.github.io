3D Printing Services

How to submit your print:

Either fill out this form: here

OR email the employees: employee_a@utmakersociety.org employee_b@utmakersociety.org

Either way, please make sure you include the following info:

  • Reason for the print: Senior Capstone, Freshman Design, Personal, etc.
  • What filament would you like us to use. Our full list is available here
  • Attach each part as seperate files, all saved as STL.
  • Dimensions and units of each part included.

About Us

The Maker Space is a workshop for UToledo students. The Maker Space maintains tools and machines for student use on academic and personal projects. We aim to give students hand-on experience applying classroom concepts.
